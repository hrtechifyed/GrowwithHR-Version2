# GrowWithHR v0.20.0 — Integrated report and industry profiles

This corrective release addresses defects identified after the M4 production deployment.

## Report integration

- M4 explainability, priority actions, law cards, upcoming triggers and the appendix now participate in the same report sequence.
- The table of contents is rebuilt after all sections are rendered.
- Page numbers cover the complete advisory rather than stopping before the M4 appendix.
- Important Information is moved behind the substantive M4 sections and before the closing page.
- The summary includes applicable, review-required, needs-information and not-currently-triggered counts.

## Evidence integrity

- Total employee count no longer substitutes for factory or blue-collar worker count.
- Factories Act and Standing Orders analysis require an independently reported worker population.
- Manufacturing activity and power usage must be confirmed before selecting the 10-worker or 20-worker factory threshold.

## Industry-adaptive assessment

- Manufacturing and plant profiles ask about production activity, factory workers, women employees, power usage, shifts and night work.
- BPO, ITES and contact-centre profiles ask about rotational/night shifts, women on night shifts, transport and security controls.
- Software and SaaS profiles focus on workforce categories, client-site work and overseas engagement.
- Factory-specific questions are not shown for software organisations unless a manufacturing profile is selected.

## Dark report

- The dark page background is true black.
- Panels use neutral charcoal rather than blue-tinted navy.
- HRTechify gold remains the accent colour.

## Safeguards

- Evidence completeness is not presented as legal certainty.
- No blended compliance or legal-confidence score is introduced.
- Official legislation links remain law-specific.
