# GrowWithHR Implementation Resources

These editable CSV files are starting points linked from GrowWithHR recommendations:

- `compliance-calendar-template.csv`
- `employee-document-checklist.csv`
- `employee-onboarding-checklist.csv`
- `policy-register-template.csv`
- `statutory-registration-tracker.csv`
- `contractor-due-diligence-checklist.csv`
- `hr-action-plan-template.csv`

## Use

Download a template, replace the examples with organisation-specific information, assign accountable owners and review it on an agreed cadence.

## Important limitation

The templates do not determine legal applicability, certify compliance, replace statutory forms or constitute legal, tax, payroll, accounting or employment advice. Verify current requirements with official authorities and qualified professionals before relying on any template.

Do not store passwords, OAuth credentials, government-portal credentials or unnecessary sensitive personal information in these files.
