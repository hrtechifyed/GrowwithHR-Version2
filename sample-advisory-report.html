COMPLIANCE

Illustrative Compliance Review Topics

These examples demonstrate how potentially relevant topics may be presented. They are not a legal determination and require organisation-specific confirmation.

🟠 Mandatory Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) Social Security

Why this appears

The organisation has crossed the statutory employee threshold for EPF applicability.

Business Impact

Payroll delays, investor due diligence observations and employee dissatisfaction.

Legal Impact

Interest, damages and recovery proceedings under applicable legislation.

🟠 Mandatory Employees' State Insurance (ESIC) Employee Welfare

Why this appears

Eligible employees fall within ESIC coverage based on workforce profile.

Business Impact

Improves employee welfare compliance and reduces operational risk.

Legal Impact

Non-compliance may result in contribution recovery, penalties and inspections.

🟠 Mandatory POSH Compliance Workplace Safety

Why this appears

Every eligible employer must establish preventive mechanisms against workplace sexual harassment.

Business Impact

Strengthens organisational culture and employee confidence.

Legal Impact

Monetary penalties and reputational consequences for repeated violations.
