Privacy

Assessment responses are used to generate the advisory in the browser session. The tool is designed around data minimisation, purpose limitation and transparency principles aligned to GDPR guidance and the European Commission data protection framework.

  • Collect only information needed for the advisory.
  • Avoid selling visitor data.
  • Use email/name capture only to identify the advisory recipient.
  • Encourage users not to enter confidential personal employee data.
